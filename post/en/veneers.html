Dental Veneers

The dental veneer are fine porcelain or composite prosthesis that stick on the anterior surface of the teeth, providing a natural and attractive appearance. Dental veneers are used to correct fractured, stained, misaligned, worn, unequal or abnormal teeth.

The most common material used in the manufacture of dental veneers are resin and porcelain. Both types can be manufactured by prosthodontist in the denture laboratory. The veneers are bonded to the teeth with resinous cement.

These veneers are indicated to improve the aesthetics of the teeth that present some of the following problems:

  • Dental caries.
  • Fractured teeth.
  • Diastemas.
  • Color change by medications such as tetracycline.
  • Conical form teeth.

The dental veneers mainly seek to imitate the color of the other teeth, to give a natural look to your smile. You should know this if you are interested in one of the most chosen treatments, which are:

  • Cover teeth fractures
    If a person is banged on a tooth and cut into a piece, or if they have a tooth damaged by use, they can put a dental veneer and cover the tooth to see it as new.
  • Cover interdental spaces
    If a person is banged on a tooth and cut into a piece, or if they have a tooth damaged by use, they can put a dental veneer and cover the tooth to see it as new.
  • Correct the tooth form
    If a tooth is too short, has a strange outgoing on the one hand, or is misaligned with the rest, it is possible to construct veneers to cover the teeth in question and thus fit the desired shape.
  • Enlarge the teeth
    Sometimes we have teeth that are too small compared to others, or a denture in general formed by small teeth that when smiling make it look more gum that tooth. Dental veneers could correct the problem of small teeth.
  • Teeth whitening
    It is one of the most effective treatments for tooth whitening, because you really do not have to work on the tooth, but cover it with a whiter tooth veneer.
